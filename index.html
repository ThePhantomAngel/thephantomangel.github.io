The Phantom Angel is a purpose built LiPo battery discharger especially designed for Phantom 2 & 3 batteries.

Every Phantom needs its angel


Most Phantom pilots are reducing their overall flight time without even knowing it.

How? By storing their batteries fully charged when not in use.

Manufacturers (including DJI) state that LiPo batteries should be stored at ~50% charge if left for more than 24 hours.

DJI’s auto-discharge function takes at least 3 days to discharge - that’s not good enough.

The Phantom Angel is a custom made device that will safely prepare your Phantom batteries for storage.
  • Optimized for fast but safe discharge (25min)
  • Customized circuitry to protect your battery
  • Designed to accommodate essential heat dissipation
  • Built with high quality materials for maximum durability

And a few frequently asked questions:

  • How much will the Angel discharge my batteries?
     Depending on the condition of the battery it will discharge to around 60-70%

  • How long will it take?
     A fully charged battery will discharge in about 25 minutes

  • Does it work on both Phantom 2 and Phantom 3 batteries?
     The Angel works on either P2 or P3 batteries, depending on the version of the angel you've ordered

  • What does DJI say about all this?
     DJI themselves recommend storing batteries discharged to around 50%

  • Does it work on normal LiPos?
     Sure. Depending on the version of the Angle you have it works on either 3S or 4S LiPos (just adjust the connector)
