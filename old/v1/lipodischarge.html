Phantom Angel - Dedicated 3S and 4S LiPo storage discharger

The Phantom Angel is a purpose built LiPo battery storage discharger for 3S and 4S LiPos.



A high quality good value solution to a common problem


LiPo batteries need good care in order to maintain their performance - and part of this is never storing fully charged for more than a day or so.

Manufacturers require that their batteries are stored at 60% charge to prolong their life and performance.

The Phantom Angel is a custom made device that will safely prepare your LiPo batteries for storage.
  • Custom circuitry designed for optimal storage of LiPo batteries
  • No expenses spared high quality build
  • Special care for appropriate heat dissipation and long life


About...


I’ve been an RC and FPV addict for two decades now. Battery performance and maintenance has been hugely important for me, especially for long range FPV flights so over the years I’ve spent countless hours learning how to care for LiPos properly.

I recently got hold of a DJI Phantom and was shocked that there’s no easy way to store its batteries properly so I went ahead and built a dedicated device for my own use - not only for Phantoms but for any 3S or 4S LiPos I needed to store.

Then, hearing good feedback from friends and fellow pilots I’m making it available to whoever will find it useful.
